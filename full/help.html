How to use JS Timer

JS Timer is a countdown timer for use in conference presetations, symposia, etc. It displays a countdown timer along with a graphical representation of the time remaining. It can break a session into "Presentation" and "Q&A" sections.

To start the timer, click on a preset in the left-hand control strip, or enter your desired time (in minutes) in the "Custom timer" section and click "Start timer" to start your custom countdown timing. To stop the timer at any point, simply click inside the timer area in the center of the window.

Click the "Configure" button to configure your own custom presets.

Shortcut keys

  • Number keys 1-9: Trigger up to nine timer presets
  • C key: Show the Time-of-Day clock
  • Space bar: Pause or restart the timer
Note: Only the Space shortcut is active when the timer is running.
